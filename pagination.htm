Pagination

There are two approaches to Pagination: a group of links that navigate to static pages and a group of buttons that load paged data into a resource. The group of links, a navigation list, is straightforward; however, the group of buttons is a little more complex, because it is more like a tablist but does not meet all the requirements of a Tab. The primary difference is that where the navigation uses aria-current, the button group uses aria-selected.
